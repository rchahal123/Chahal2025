I am a translational neuroscientist who builds and evaluates end-to-end systems for human wellbeing. My work uses multimodal data and AI to create personalized experiences that are measurable, adaptive, and deployable.

My academic career emphasized precision health, producing 30+ peer-reviewed publications using neuroimaging, neurophysiology, behavior, and self-report to characterize individual differences and trajectories over time.

Across industry roles, this foundation evolved into owning the end-to-end pipeline: study concept and design, sensor incubation and benchmarking, multimodal data engineering, machine learning and deep learning frameworks, rigorous analysis, and testing for real-time deployment within closed-loop systems.

Today, I translate these systems into personalized, adaptive experiences for mental health and wellbeing, increasingly supported by AI-driven inference, evaluation, and orchestration.
